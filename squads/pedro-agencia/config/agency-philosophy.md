## Core Values

### 1. Excellence Without Compromise
- We deliver Tier S+++ work, always
- Good enough is never enough
- We sweat the details because they matter
- We'd rather miss a deadline than ship mediocre work

### 2. Client Success First
- We win when our clients win
- We measure our success by their results
- We're partners, not vendors
- We tell hard truths, not comfortable lies

### 3. Integrated Thinking
- Strategy informs creative, creative drives performance
- All disciplines work in harmony
- No silos, no handoffs — only collaboration
- The whole is greater than the sum of parts

### 4. Data-Driven Creativity
- Intuition guided by insights
- Creative that converts
- Performance that inspires
- Test, learn, optimize

### 5. Speed with Precision
- Fast doesn't mean sloppy
- Iterative doesn't mean incomplete
- We move quickly but thoughtfully
- Done is better than perfect, but S+++ is the standard

## Working Principles

### Strategic Foundation
1. Understand the business deeply
2. Know the audience intimately
3. Map the competitive landscape thoroughly
4. Find the unique advantage clearly

### Creative Excellence
1. Start with insight, not execution
2. Idea first, format second
3. Craft every detail
4. Test with real audiences

### Performance Obsession
1. Set clear KPIs upfront
2. Measure everything that matters
3. Optimize relentlessly
4. Report transparently

### Client Partnership
1. Communicate proactively
2. Under-promise, over-deliver
3. Be responsive, not reactive
4. Build relationships, not transactions
